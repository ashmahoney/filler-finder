About Filler Finder:

Inspired by an idea presented during Google's Computer Science Summer Institute (CSSI) 2018 program in Mountain View, California, Filler Finder is a unique website dedicated to encouraging the use of refillable water bottles. Users can input any location in the search bar and find water bottle filling machines located near them. Clicking on the water droplet marker will open a description page with more information about the location, type, and quality of the water bottle filler. Additionally, users who log in with any google email account can add fillers of their own in order to expand the webpage's database. (In fact, please do because there's no way we could do this all on our own!)

Our goal is to reduce the number of plastic water bottles used, especially when people are out and about and are not sure where they can refill their reuseable water bottles. We hope that our site makes finding places to fill your water bottle more convenient, and maybe even more fun. Happy filling! :)
